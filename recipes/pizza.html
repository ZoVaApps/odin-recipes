Pizza

The Worlds Best Pizza

I have been making homemade pizza for years and years, and have lots of tips and tricks to share.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour for rolling and shaping dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the toppings
  5. Cook the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
